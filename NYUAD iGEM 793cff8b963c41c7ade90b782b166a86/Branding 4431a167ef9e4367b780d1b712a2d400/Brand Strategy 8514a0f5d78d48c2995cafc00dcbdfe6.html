Brand Strategy

What we do:

iGEM gives students the opportunity to tackle everyday issues by pushing the boundaries of synthetic biology.

Goals: Why we do what we do

  • iGEM
  • To expand what there is to know about molecuar/synthetic biology

How we do what we do:

  • Introduced to a problem area to engage with and we bought into it (via iGEM)
    • Multidisciplinary teams work together to design, build, test, and measure a system of their own design using interchangeable biological parts and standard molecular biology techniques. Modern problems require modern solutions.
  • Needed an environment that sustained it, enter NYUAD that provided the multidisciplinary environment, resources, leading students, and aligned perfectly for the ingredients required
    • NYUAD was established with a common belief in the value of a liberal arts education, concurrence on the benefits a research university brings to the society that sustains it, a conviction that interaction with new ideas and people who are different is valuable and necessary, and a commitment to educating students who are true citizens of the world.

Identity:

  • global leaders that want to solve problems as an interdisciplinary team
  • Research team
  • Multidisciplinary global students that utilize synthetic biology (contemporary, innovative technology)
  • Product Development as an end goal, to deliver a solution

Personality:

  • Minimalistic
  • Dynamic, adaptable
  • Cheerful
  • Conscientious
  • Young but professional
  • Ambitious
  • Green - sustainable, natural, and the color
  • Unique
  • Quirky

Design brief for NYUAD iGEM team logo:

  • Our logo would communicate how these forces are aligned in the NYUAD iGEM group to deliver solutions using synthetical biological methods
