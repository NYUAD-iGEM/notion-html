Equipment:

  • Analyzer consists of: Microcontroller, Peltier electrical heater + aluminium heating plate + micro-thermistor, servo motor for disk actuation, magnet, LED + Optical fiber for fluoroscence excitation, optical sensor (TCS34725, Digi-Key) + optical fiber for detection, touch screen LCD display, connectivity to smartphone.
  • The custom made disk consists of a lysate inlet and multiple chambers for washing and reaction steps. Magnetic beads are used to bind to DNA and propagate it
